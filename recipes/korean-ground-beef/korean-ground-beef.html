Korean Ground Beef Bowl

Description

This is a must have recipe that has saved countless weeknights from McDisasters.

Ingredients

Make sure to use high-quality soy sauce and short or medium grain rice. It really makes a difference in flavor and texture.

  • 1 Pound 90% lean ground beef
  • 3 Garlic cloves minced
  • 1/4 Cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 Cup soy sauce
  • 1 Teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 Teaspoon fresh minced ginger
  • 1/4 Teaspoon gochugaru (Korean red pepper flakes)
  • 1/2 Onion grated on box grater
  • 2 Cups hot cooked white rice
  • Sliced green onions and sesame seeds for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large skillet cook the ground beef and garlic breaking it into crumbles over medium heat until no longer pink.
  2. In a small bowl whisk brown sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, grated onion, red pepper flakes and pepper. Pour over the ground beef and let simmer for another minute or two.
  3. Serve over hot rice and garnish with green onions and sesame seeds.

    4. Bonus: If you have gochujang and roasted seaweed, they're both great for this dish to add some flare.
