Chicken Parmesan

Description

This is a recipe passed down from generation to generation, just make sure not to put too many onions in the sauce...

Ingredients

Listen up, the ingredients are simple... Just don't be a wise guy about it.

  • 4 Boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 Cup all purpose flour
  • 1 Tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon pepper
  • 1 Teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 1 Cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1 Cup italian style bread crumbs
  • 3/4 Cup freshly grated parmesan cheese divided
  • 1/4 Cup olive oil
  • 1/4 Cup butter
  • 2 Cups marinara sauce
  • 8 Ounces fresh mozzarella
  • 1/4 Cup chopped fresh basil
